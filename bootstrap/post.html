Komal

I recently visited Kap & Kissa Café, and it was an absolute joy! The whimsical charm of the place instantly puts a smile on your face. The menu is a delightful mix of traditional and playful, with a special focus on momos that left me wanting more. I tried the Khushmijaaz Tandoori Momos, and they were bursting with flavor and fun. The Khatarnak Aloo Tikki were perfectly cooked, with a mischievous twist that made them irresistible. For dessert,Malaidar Rasgulla were an innovative and delicious fusion. The ambiance is cozy and inviting, with a touch of whimsy that makes it a perfect spot to relax and unwind. The staff is friendly and attentive, adding to the overall fantastic experience. Kap & Kissa Café is a must-visit for anyone looking for a unique and enjoyable culinary adventure. The playful names and delicious dishes create an unforgettable dining experience. Highly recommend!

Aashish

As someone who loves exploring unique eateries, Kap & Kissa Café exceeded my expectations. From the moment I walked in, the cozy and whimsical ambiance made me feel right at home. The staff greeted me warmly and were quick to offer recommendations. I couldn't resist the Tukde-Tukde Burger and packed with joy in every bite. The British Falavar was an exciting mix of flavors, that kept my taste buds entertained. For a sweet ending, Badam aur Pista Ka Shahi Tukda were a delightful treat, combining the richness of Badam with the classic chocolate texture. The café’s playful menu names and creative dishes make it a standout. Whether you’re there for a quick snack or a leisurely meal, Kap & Kissa Café promises a fun and flavorful escape. It’s the perfect spot to hang out with friends or even enjoy some quiet time with a good book. Highly recommend for a quirky and enjoyable experience!
